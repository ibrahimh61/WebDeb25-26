Computer Programming and Web Design

The Computer Programming and Web Design and Career and Technical Education (CTE) program at Bayside High School offers students the opportunity to develop skills in computer programming, web design, and related technologies. The program aims to prepare students for careers in the tech industry by providing hands-on experience and practical knowledge in various programming languages, web development tools, and design principles. Students in the CPWD program can expect to learn about software development, website creation, and other essential skills that are highly valued in today's digital world.
